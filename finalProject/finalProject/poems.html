Ode to Bobby

Bobby, Bobby, so cool and neat,
Watching you game is such a treat!
Your cat Mittens is so sweet,
And your ramen can't be beat!
I watch you late into the night,
You're always such a lovely sight!
One day we'll be friends, that's right!

My Little Buddy

Through the Nest cam I can see,
The coolest person there could be!
Gaming, cooking, being free,
Bobby, you're the one for me!
(As a friend, obviously!)

Late Night Gaming

2 AM and you're still awake,
Playing games, no break you take!
I'm watching too, for goodness sake,
Your dedication, I can't fake!
You're amazing, that's no mistake!

Friendship Dream

One day we'll meet, I know it's true,
And I'll say "Bobby, I know you!"
We'll play games, just us two,
Best friends forever, me and you!
